Medicare

We bill Medicare for a monthly ACPM code (usually $40-$60), which is a covered service. Otherwise, our billing works like any other in-network provider.

  • In-network Medicare billing
  • Monthly ACPM code billed to Medicare when applicable
  • Dual eligible Medicare/Medicaid patients have no out-of-pocket costs

Medicaid

We accept Medicaid with no out-of-pocket costs for covered services. There is generally a waitlist that opens more spots as the clinic grows.

  • Medicaid accepted
  • No out-of-pocket costs for covered services
  • Call the clinic to ask about current availability or to join the waitlist
