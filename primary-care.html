Comprehensive primary care

Comprehensive primary care, built around relationships and better access.

Premonition Health offers adult primary care with more time, easier communication, and thoughtful coordination. The goal is simple: help you get the care you need without feeling rushed, lost, or left on your own.

Who this is for

  • Adults who want a stronger relationship with their clinician
  • People managing one or more ongoing health conditions
  • Patients who want more time during visits
  • Busy people who value faster access and easier follow-up
  • Anyone looking for more coordinated, individualized care

Flexible communication

When appropriate, you can reach the clinic by text, phone, email, or video. The clinic helps guide you to the right format based on your question and what kind of care you need.

What primary care includes

Specific services, clearly explained

Preventive care

Annual visits, screenings, wellness review, and risk reduction planning.

Chronic disease care

Support for high blood pressure, diabetes, thyroid disease, and more.

Acute visits

Care for common illnesses, new symptoms, and problems that need attention now.

Medication management

Thoughtful review, changes, monitoring, and refill support.

Care coordination

Referrals, records review, and help connecting the dots across your healthcare.

Follow-up access

Flexible communication between visits when it is appropriate and useful.
