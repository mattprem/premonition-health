Meet the team

Meet the team behind Premonition Health

Good primary care is personal. The clinic is built to provide thoughtful medical care, clear communication, and a patient experience that feels supportive from the first conversation forward.

MB

Matt Bezzant, MD

Internal Medicine

Dr. Bezzant founded Premonition Health to create a more relational, practical model of primary care. His focus is comprehensive adult medicine, thoughtful care coordination, and building a clinic where patients have the time and access they need.

Background

Dr. Bezzant earned his medical degree from the University of Colorado School of Medicine and completed an Air Force residency in internal medicine in San Antonio. He later served as an active duty physician before building Premonition Health in Wichita.

How he approaches care

Primary care works better when patients have time, trust, and a clinician who can help connect the dots.

  • More listening
  • Clearer explanations
  • Plans built around the individual
  • Coordination when care gets complex
